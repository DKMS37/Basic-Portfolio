About Me

Don Sangalang also know for his nicknames "DK", and "King" is currently a Full-stack Web Developer Student at UCLA. He studied Arts and Designs such as Painting, Drawing, Photoshop and Maya softwares at Glendale Community College. He worked in a retail Computer Company for almost five years and learned how to build Computers from scratch.

Raised and Surrounded with a Family of Artists, He has been in many Art Exhibits together with his Dad, older Brother and Sister and sold most of his artworks. He is also a Freelance Photographer and Videographer and did Wedding and Anniversary Events.

Originally born in one of the seven thousand one hundred seven Pacific Islands of the Philippines, He is now Happily Married with no pets such as Dog, Cat, etc. and currently residing in Los Angeles California.
